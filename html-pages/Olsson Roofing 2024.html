Safetyfest

Our President, Mike Reynolds, delivered a profound and impactful speech putting our workforce front and center. His words painted a vivid image of our company's legacy, character, and the role of each employee in shaping it.

  • With Olsson's journey spanning over a century, Reynolds attributed our staying power and customer loyalty to the exceptional safety standards we maintain. He presented an insightful perspective that safety is not just a feature we offer, but a valuable product that our customers deliberately invest in. This further underlined the connection between our unmatched capabilities and unwavering commitment to safety.

    Emphasizing that Olsson refuses to compromise by resorting to shortcuts, Reynolds affirmed our dedication to delivering not just a quality roof, but a safely constructed one. Acknowledging the inherent risks associated with the roofing profession, he expressed our primary goal: the safe return of all employees to their loved ones at the end of each workday.

    Beyond just enforcing safety protocols, Reynolds highlighted the need for ongoing education and training for our employees. By ensuring they are equipped with these essential resources, we're able to promote a culture of safety. He concluded by drawing a parallel between the importance of the Safetyfest and our high-profile projects, once again emphasizing that safety isn’t just a policy, but the backbone of our operation.

Our Services

  • New Construction

    Our project management team provides our clients safe, timely, reliable information allowing a mutual understanding of the project through project closeout. Working with Olsson Roofing Company ensures that your building’s roof is done right the first time.

    Make It

  • Roof Replacement

    Olsson Roofing delivers superior commercial roof replacement through comprehensive digital analysis, custom-engineered solutions, and state-of-the-art installation methods. Our certified technicians utilize premium materials and advanced technology while providing real-time project updates, ensuring minimal business disruption and maximum long-term protection backed by industry-leading warranties.

    Make It

  • ROOF RECOVER / OVERLAY

    A roof recover/overlay can be a cost-effective alternative when your roof is too worn for repairs but funds aren't available for full replacement. Before proceeding, we perform test cuts to ensure the existing materials are dry and in suitable condition, allowing you to save on labor and insulation costs by keeping the old roofing materials in place.

    Make It

  • Architectural Metal Systems

    Metal Edge, a division of Olsson Roofing Company, specializes in designing, fabricating, and installing architectural sheet metal systems for commercial projects. Their services include metal wall panels, roof systems, and custom sheet metal components, with 95% of materials produced in-house by experts with over 70 years of combined experience. They are certified installers for major industry suppliers such as Kingspan, Morin, and Petersen Aluminum.

  • Roofing Service & Repair

    We offer comprehensive roof repair services for various systems, including single-ply (EPDM, TPO, PVC), modified bitumen, built-up roofing (BUR), and metal roofs. Our experienced team provides prompt leak investigations, moisture surveys, and tailored repair or restoration proposals to address issues such as damaged membranes, faulty flashings, and drainage problems.

    Click Here

  • Green Roofing

    Green roofs, also known as "living roofs," offer benefits ranging from increased property value and energy savings to environmental advantages like rainwater absorption and wildlife habitat creation. Olsson Roofing Company has extensive experience with all types of green roofs and can provide consultation and installation for intensive, extensive, or technology-enhanced roofing systems.

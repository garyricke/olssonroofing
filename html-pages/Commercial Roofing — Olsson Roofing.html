Safetyfest 2023

Our President, Mike Reynolds, delivered a profound and impactful speech at this year's Safetyfest, putting our workforce front and center. His words painted a vivid image of our company's legacy, character, and the role of each employee in shaping it.

With Olsson's journey spanning over a century, Reynolds attributed our staying power and customer loyalty to the exceptional safety standards we maintain. He presented an insightful perspective that safety is not just a feature we offer, but a valuable product that our customers deliberately invest in. This further underlined the connection between our unmatched capabilities and unwavering commitment to safety.

Emphasizing that Olsson refuses to compromise by resorting to shortcuts, Reynolds affirmed our dedication to delivering not just a quality roof, but a safely constructed one. Acknowledging the inherent risks associated with the roofing profession, he expressed our primary goal: the safe return of all employees to their loved ones at the end of each workday.

Beyond just enforcing safety protocols, Reynolds highlighted the need for ongoing education and training for our employees. By ensuring they are equipped with these essential resources, we're able to promote a culture of safety. He concluded by drawing a parallel between the importance of the Safetyfest and our high-profile projects, once again emphasizing that safety isn’t just a policy, but the backbone of our operation.

Roof Recover / Overlay

When a roof system becomes too worn for repair or restoration, there are not always funds available for a roof replacement.

Before we consider this option, test cuts need to be performed in order to determine if the old roofing materials are free of moisture and are still in decent condition.

If this is your situation, you may want to consider a roof recover/overlay. This will allow you to leave the old roofing materials in place and thus save on labor and insulation costs.

Green Roofing

Also known as “living roofs,” green roofs provide a number of key benefits. For building owners and operators, they increase real estate value, reduce energy consumption, and contribute to LEED points. For the environment, they absorb rainwater, provide insulation, create habitat for wildlife, and help to lower air temperatures.

Olsson has experience with every kind of green roof under the sun! We can assist you whether you’re considering an intensive green roof, an extensive green roof, or simply adding some piece of environmental technology (e.g., solar panels) to your existing roofing system. Olsson Roofing Company is qualified to provide consultation and installation of the green roof you’ve been imagining.

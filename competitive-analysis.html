Marketing Spend Insight
The 10.5:1 ROI on Digital Spend
The key number: High-end competitors in the Chicago commercial roofing market achieve an estimated 10.5:1 return on their digital marketing investment.

What This Means in Practice

A competitor spending $20,000/month on SEO, PPC, and Local Service Ads is likely generating approximately $210,000+ in monthly revenue directly attributable to digital channels. Over a year, a $240,000 marketing investment compounds into $2.5M+ in digitally-sourced revenue.

Why the ROI Is This High for Commercial Roofing

  • High average contract values: A single commercial reroof can be $500k–$2M+. One closed deal from a digital lead pays for months of spend.
  • Service/repair volume: 4,000 service calls/year at an average ticket of $2,000–$5,000 each is $8M–$20M in revenue alone — and digital drives emergency service discovery.
  • Maintenance contract stickiness: A facility manager who finds a roofer via Google tends to stay. LTV of a commercial client is 5–15 years.

What This Means for Olsson

Olsson already has the operational capacity to absorb more leads — 51 trucks, 200+ annual projects, 4,000 service calls. The constraint isn't delivery; it's digital discovery. Even a conservative 8:1 ROI on a $150k/year digital investment would represent $1.2M in incremental digitally-sourced revenue.

The Lead Volume Math

At $320 per qualified commercial lead (2026 estimate), closing 10–15 commercial projects per month requires generating 50–100+ qualified leads monthly — assuming a 15–20% close rate on commercial bids.

Conservative (10 jobs/mo)
  • ~50 qualified leads needed
  • $16,000/mo in lead generation
  • $192,000/yr lead gen budget
Aggressive (20 jobs/mo)
  • ~100+ qualified leads needed
  • $32,000/mo in lead generation
  • $384,000/yr lead gen budget

How to Reduce CPL

  • SEO and content: Organic leads have near-zero marginal cost once content is live. A keyword-ranked page can generate leads for 3–5 years.
  • Review volume: High review counts reduce paid acquisition costs — Google rewards organic trust signals with better ad Quality Scores.
  • Referral systems: Facility manager and GC referrals have $0 CPL. Olsson's testimonial network is already primed for this.
  • Email marketing to existing clients: Quarterly maintenance touches to existing clients have the lowest CPL of any channel for repeat commercial work.

How Aggressive Competitors Allocate Paid Budget

For a $20,000/month paid budget in the Chicago commercial roofing market:

  • Google Local Service Ads — $6,000/mo (30%): "Google Guaranteed" badge at the very top of results. Pay per verified lead, not per click. Extremely effective for emergency service calls.
  • Traditional PPC (Google Ads) — $13,000/mo (65%): Targets commercial intent keywords, retargeting, and competitor brand terms. Includes search + display + remarketing.
  • Other (Bing, LinkedIn, review platforms) — $1,000/mo (5%): LinkedIn particularly effective for reaching facility managers and property decision-makers in the B2B commercial space.

Why LSA Matters for Olsson Specifically

Olsson's 4,000+ service calls/year and 24/7 emergency response are exactly what LSA buyers are searching for. The "Google Guaranteed" badge directly addresses the trust gap that Olsson has in external reviews. A facility manager searching "emergency roof repair" at 2am will see the LSA listing first — and the Google Guaranteed badge substitutes for review volume in that moment.
